FilmoPedia

FilmoPedia is for movie lovers. It was built to find out which movie is best for the user to view. This application searches for detailed movie information and movies from the top 50 movies list. It also finds each movie's original movie trailers.

Salsa Quiz App

The Salsa Quiz App is for salsa dance enthusiasts. It was built as a fun trivia for users to test their knowledge of salsa dance. This app asks questions about salsa dance style/history; it also gives users feedback on questions they answered right or wrong. In the end, they get a score on their salsa knowledge.

FlashCards App

FlashCards App is for students. It was built to quickly learn and study a list of terms and definitions. It features an intuitive interface that allows users to study, edit, add, and delete flashcards/decks. In addition, it has a unique Flashcard flip animation triggered upon pressing the flip card button.
